About

Today, where you live says more about your health than the genes you were born with. Your zip code is not just a geographical tag; it is a significant indicator of your health. Your location is a representation of your socio-environmental landscape, thus portraying your resource availability and relevant risk factors. PlacePredicts reports disease trends in addition to variables that capture environmental and social determinants of health across the nation. This data will enable practitioners to offer more effective care, thus bridging health inequity.
