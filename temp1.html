How It Works

PlacePredicts uses publicly available datasets compiled by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency and the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention to estimate county level (n=3141) disease trends, environmental hazards, and socio-environmental measures across the nation. Information explaining the variables is published elsewhere (DATA.GOV and CDC.GOV). Clinical practitioners can access electronic health records of their patients and locate their respective zip codes. They can then simply search these zip codes in the database and retrieve specific demographic, environmental, and disease incidence information. This insight can help aid practitioners in providing more personalized health suggestions.
